What is Lemonade?

Lemonade is open-source, free, and runs on regular PC hardware — no special cloud account needed.

Four Modalities. One Platform.

Lemonade is not just a chatbot — it is a full local AI platform covering four modalities.

🗣️
Speech to Text
Talk into your mic and get a transcript in seconds. Powered by Whisper, running on the NPU.
🧠
Text Generation
Chat, summarize, and analyze text — like ChatGPT but running entirely on your machine. Powered by an LLM.
🔊
Text to Speech
Turn any written text into natural, lifelike voice output. Powered by Kokoro, running on the CPU.
🎨
Image Generation
Describe an image in words and watch it appear. Powered by Stable Diffusion, running on your GPU.

All four can run simultaneously on the same PC.

🎪 The COMPUTEX Demo — 5 Acts

At COMPUTEX 2026, AMD ran a fully automated 5-act live demo on a single PC.

1

🎤 Act 1: Speech In

Whisper converts voice to text on the NPU. CPU and GPU sit completely idle.

2

🧠 Act 2: Brain Thinks

The DeepSeek LLM processes the text. NPU and iGPU work simultaneously in Hybrid mode.

3

🔊 Act 3: Speech Out

Kokoro speaks the response aloud. It runs on the CPU while NPU and GPU are free for other tasks.

4

🎨 Act 4: Image Created

Stable Diffusion generates a visual from the conversation. The GPU takes over at full power.

5

💬 Act 5: AI Debate

Five different AI models argue a topic simultaneously on the GPU. All at once, on the same laptop.

Three chips. Five AI tasks. One laptop. Zero cloud.

Key Insight: Drop-in ChatGPT Replacement

Lemonade exposes an API at http://localhost:13305 that is fully compatible with OpenAI. Any app built for ChatGPT works with Lemonade — just change one URL.

// Before — using cloud AI
base_url = "https://api.openai.com/v1"
// After — using Lemonade
base_url = "http://localhost:13305/api/v0/openai"
✓ Everything else in your code stays exactly the same.
